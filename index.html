About

Kuan-I Gary Chung (鍾冠毅, jhōng guàn yì) is a senior engineer at Wistron Corporation and a visiting researcher at MIT CSAIL. His research interest spans the field of machine learning, computer vision, medical imaging analysis, and visualization. He received his MS and BS in Applied Mathematics from National Sun Yat-sen University, Taiwan in 2018 and 2016. He joined Wistron in 2018, focusing on data science and machine learning.
